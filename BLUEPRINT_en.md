```
[ Audio / Language / Story ]
          │
          ▼
        Vrew

   timecodes assigned
          │
          ▼
  ┌─────────────────────────────┐
  │   SRT = Performance Score   │
  │  sequence · timecode · text  │
  └─────────────────────────────┘
          │
    ┌─────┼─────┬──────────┐
    ▼     ▼     ▼          ▼
DaVinci  TTS  LLM +      Auto
Resolve       AI char    video gen
 video   voice  acting·   one source
```

SRT is the HUB. Write the words and the timecodes. The tools perform the rest.

## Lineage — A Structure That Was Always There

SRT Performance Score is not an invention. It is a **discovery** of a structure that has existed across every genre, for centuries.

| Form | Design of Time |
|------|----------------|
| Kamishibai | The silence between picture and narration |
| Four-panel manga | The rhythm of setup, development, turn, punchline |
| Rakugo | The pause of *"Eeeh…"* |
| Hip-hop | Call and response |
| Techno | Build and drop |
| VJ lighting | The lighting cue |
| YouTube viral | The design of the first 3 seconds |
| Music video | The rhythm cut |
| Mashup | The timing of the transition |
| **SRT** | **← The smallest unit of all of the above** |

All of them are the same thing: **the design of tension and release across time.** SRT encodes that design into the smallest form a machine can read.

## Who This Is For

This project is not for people who are already satisfied.

- **You love MVs but don't know how to make one.**
  You feel the rhythm. The cuts play in your head. You just don't know the tools.

- **You hear mashups in your head but can't read music.**
  You hear combinations no one has heard before. The score makes them visible.

- **You have stories that never become images.**
  The story exists. It just needs a protocol to become time.

- **I can create AI-generated manga at a human level, but I've developed a fear of creating videos.**
I can write storyboards. I just need to change my habit of doing everything with AI.

- **You're a normal programmer who only works locally.**
  GitHub feels scary (so did it for this project's author). But you think structurally — and that is the real work.

> The people who see the concept — not the interface —
> are the ones who find what everyone else missed.

## What This Project Is Reaching For

MIDI has been in use for decades.

Why? Because it separated the *meaning* of sound from the sound itself. It became the HUB that connects every device. That is why it has lasted.

SRT can do the same thing. And it has one advantage MIDI does not.

**A human can read it.**

MIDI is binary. No one reads it directly. But SRT is plain text. Anyone can write it. Edit it. Version-control it on GitHub.

The score was always there. It just needed a name.
