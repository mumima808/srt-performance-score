# STORY.md — A Log of Discovery

**Notes from the day I first touched Vrew, 2026**

---

## The First Unease

Video editing has long carried assumptions that went unquestioned.

Scanning the timeline. Guessing "somewhere around here."
Placing the cursor a little earlier. Playing it back again and again.
There was no other way to find the words you were looking for.

That felt normal. That was just how it worked.

---

## The Moment It Clicked

When I opened Vrew, I saw blocks stacked vertically on the screen.

At each pause in the audio, the words were right there — written out.
Not as a waveform. Not as a timeline. As text.

*"The words are visible."*

That was all. But that was enough to change everything.
No more hunting. Just read.
Find the word you want to fix with your eyes, and touch it.

---

## What the Numbers Showed

I fed in 15 minutes of continuous footage.
75 seconds later, blocks of roughly 28 characters were lined up vertically.

For the first time, I could see the whole picture at once.
A feeling I had never had while staring at a timeline.

---

## What Got Inverted

Traditional editing meant *moving along a time axis.*
Vrew turned it into *reading a list of words.*

This is not a UI improvement.
It is a reversal of philosophy.

On a timeline, words are buried inside audio.
In Vrew, words come first. Time is just their attribute.

---

## The Connection to SRT

This discovery changed how I see SRT files.

SRT is thought of as a subtitle format.
But what it actually is: a mapping table between words and time.

The gaps between timecodes are not blank space. They are designed silence.
Pauses between utterances, breaths, intentional beats — all recorded as numbers.

That's when I wanted to call it: the MIDI data of video.

---

## A Hope

What Vrew did is not the evolution of a single tool.

I want "words-first" to become the new standard for video editing.
In DaVinci Resolve. In Premiere. In Final Cut.
If every editing software opened with words visible first,
the place where creators burn out would fundamentally change.

This cannot stay a Vrew-only story.

---

## End of Log

This project begins from that discovery.

Too many people run out of energy learning the tools,
and never reach the point of creating.
Vrew cuts that exhaustion down dramatically.

What lies beyond the cut?
That is what this log is here to record.
